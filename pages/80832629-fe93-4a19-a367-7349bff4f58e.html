Tools for classrooms of every size and every budget

Technology is transforming what’s possible in schools—enabling innovative solutions to learning’s biggest barriers. With our suite of devices, apps, and resources, you can easily bring the power of technology to your classroom.

Spark learning with Chromebooks

Perfect for sharable class sets or 1 to 1 programs, Chromebooks are versatile devices with dynamic content and G Suite built in. They’re easy to use and simple to manage. That’s why U.S. schools have made them the #1 device.¹

Foster collaboration with G Suite for Education

70 million educators and students use G Suite for Education: a suite of free, secure productivity tools. Popular tools like Gmail and Google Docs enable easy classroom collaboration.

Engage students with digital tools

Digital tools can help teachers nurture responsible digital citizens, world explorers, and ambitious creators. Purchase discounted bundles of popular tools for common classroom needs like video-making, podcasts, and coding. Or use free apps like Google Earth, WeVideo, and Google Search.
