Quick install

Copy & paste the snippet below into your HTML page.

<!DOCTYPE html>
<html>
<head>
  <script src="https://cloud.tinymce.com/stable/tinymce.min.js"></script>
  <script>tinymce.init({ selector:'textarea' });</script>
</head>
<body>
  <textarea>Next, start a free trial!</textarea>
</body>
</html>
