jsoup discussion

If you have a question on how to use jsoup, please post in on Stack Overflow. Use the jsoup tag. Your question will be seen an answered sooner than if it is posted to the list. (And it may have already been answered.)

For general jsoup discussion, including development discussion, please join the jsoup mailing list by mailing jsoup+subscribe@googlegroups.com.

Post by sending mail to jsoup@googlegroups.com. You're welcome to post without being subscribed, but you will need to watch the topic archive to get the replies.

To avoid spam, your first email to the group will be moderated. Please be patient and send only once: as soon as a moderator reads your email it will be sent on to the group.
