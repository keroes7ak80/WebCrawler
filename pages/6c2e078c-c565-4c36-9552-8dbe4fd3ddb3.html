jsoup 1.3.2 released

2010-Aug-30 jsoup 1.3.2 has been released and is now available for download.

This is a minor release that:

  • improves the reliability of gzip response encoding detection in Connection, and
  • improves table parsing from malformed HTML.

Many thanks to those who have tested and reported issues.
